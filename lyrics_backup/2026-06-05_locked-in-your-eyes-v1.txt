[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Whispered]
I am tracing the shadows falling softly on your face
The midnight is quiet, but my heart is set to race
There is a fire in the silence when you pull me close
You are the only kind of magic that I want the most

[Verse 1]
We left the headlights fading on the gravel road
Trading the heavy world for a lighter load
I feel the static rising every time we touch
Swore I would take it easy, but I crave you too much

[Pre-Chorus]
The embers are glowing, catching the light in your hair
There is a sweet kind of danger hanging thick in the air
I lean in a little closer, breathing in your scent
Every second we are here is a moment perfectly spent

[Chorus]
Because I am completely locked in your eyes
Losing my direction under starry skies
Your fingertips are speaking words we leave unsaid
Dancing with the memories running through my head

[Echo]
